$$ f(x) = \sum^n_{i=1}x_i^2 $$

\Cref{fig:rosenbrock} shows the rosenbrock function, a uni-model optimization function.

$$ f(x) = \sum^{n-1}_{i=1} [100(x_{i+1} - x_i^2)^2 + (1-x_i)^2] $$
